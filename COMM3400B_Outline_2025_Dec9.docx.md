2025 – 2026 / Term 2
COURSE DESCRIPTION
As Pinpoint is a journey about strategize and create ideas to solve marketing communication challenges, we have come to the final course of the journey.
COMM3400 is an advance practice in public engagement that focus on developing student’s ability to engage diverse audiences, designing impactful outreach activities, and use appropriate communication platforms to promote understanding and collaboration between the client and the public.
With the skills that you have learned in other courses or through the Pinpoint practicum (COMM2100 & COMM2240), it is expected that student’s commitment to create impactful, community-connected communication.
Expected Learning Outcome
IMC is not simply about contemporary marketing communication theories but more on your acumen towards creating solutions. Therefore, as a Practicum module, it is about hands-on learning experience, and expected to achieve the following areas:
Understand client’s challenges through effective briefing, expectation alignment and feedback from different parties;
Demonstrate advanced strategic thinking in the planning of engagement activities, including clear objectives, stakeholders analysis and appropriate choice of platforms;
Create professional communication assets solving client’s challenges that is applicable for real-world application;
Strengthen skills of organization, project implementation, presentation between the students, the teachers and the professionals in the marketing industry;
Collaborate effectively in interdisciplinary teams and with external partners;

In-Class Participation and Discussion (20%)

Peer Evaluation (10%)

Individual Critiques & Presentation (25%)

Define the service featured in the IMC campaign;
Identify its Target Audience;
Study and evaluate the Communication Idea by scrutinizing the communication strategy versus its target audiences;
Analyse the success factors or its failure areas;
Create improvement of the campaign that would enhance the impact;
Language : English / Chinese
Maximum 15 ppt slides , including graphics, photos if necessary;
Presentation (10 min) is required to deliver the keynote, with written submission of the case analysis;
Written Submission (15%) + Presentation (10%)
Selected works may be showcased in JLM Pinpoint’s website & social media for publication.
Late or failure in submission will result in grade deduction.
Individual Assessment Requirement
Research methodology and data collection quality;
Audience segmentation and Depth of the analysis;
Consumer insight identification and validation;
Strategic recommendation quality and feasibility;
Use of appropriate theoretical frameworks;
Practicum Project (20%+30%+5% = 55%)
Students will form groups of 4 to work with the sponsored client;
The topic will be initiated from the client, sometimes it is ambiguous that require the students to identify the needs, while sometimes it is specific on the areas that the client would like the teams to solve;
The Project should be well thought, well executed and well presented to the client; It is a work representing your group;
It will be evaluated and feedback by the clients, lecturer and TA;
If applicable, the Project will be featured in JLM PinPoint’s website / fb page for publication;
Mid Term (20%)
The purpose of Mid-Term is to establish an initial feedback from client, which the team can choose their next steps: be it a change to a new direction or an adjustment to the existing ones; Or, simply to seek more information from the client, so a better judgement can be made;
A session will be held with Client which the teams present their initial thoughts; it’s not a full presentation, hence it’s limited to 20 minutes (max) from each team, unless client agree otherwise;
The Grading is not based on the quality of initial works, but the quality of communication towards discussion with clients, such as the clarity of the questions,  preparation of the meetings;
Final Work (30%)
A continuation and completion of IMC campaign plan & execution which supposed to take mid term review to next step;
Students are required to present the full IMC campaign recommendation to the clients for feedback and teachers for feedback & evaluation;
Full IMC plan presentation and creative demonstration is mandatory.  Full IMC plan should include: competitive and brand analysis, strategic thinking, key message, creative idea demonstration and application of IMC tools in the campaign implementation;
Budget control is also part of the evaluation. A tiny budget will be provided to the teams. Each team is required to submit a budget summary with official invoices. Details will be provided later.
Project Review (5%)
Your preparation in the Project Review leads to the Final Work is counted as an effort;
Failure to produce any evidence will be counted as not performing in the Review;

Academic Honesty and Plagiarism
Attention is drawn to University policy and regulations on honesty in academic work, and to the disciplinary guidelines and procedures applicable to breaches of such policy and regulations. Details may be found at http://www.cuhk.edu.hk/policy/academichonesty/.
With each assignment, students will be required to submit a signed declaration that they are aware of these policies, regulations, guidelines and procedures.
· In the case of group projects, all members of the group should be asked to sign the declaration, each of whom is responsible and liable to disciplinary actions, irrespective of whether he/she has signed the declaration and whether he/she has contributed, directly or indirectly, to the problematic contents.
· For assignments in the form of a computer-generated document that is principally text-based and submitted via VeriGuide, the statement, in the form of a receipt, will be issued by the system upon students’ uploading of the soft copy of the assignment.
· Students are fully aware that their work may be investigated by AI content detection software to determine originality.
· Students are fully aware of the AI approach(es) adopted in the course. In the case where some AI tools are allowed, students have made proper acknowledgment and citations as suggested by the course teacher.
Assignments without a properly signed declaration will not be graded by teachers.
Only the final version of the assignment should be submitted via VeriGuide.
The submission of a piece of work, or a part of a piece of work, for more than one purpose (e.g. to satisfy the requirements in two different courses) without declaration to this effect shall be regarded as having committed undeclared multiple submissions. It is common and acceptable to reuse a turn of phrase or a sentence or two from one’s own work; but wholesale reuse is problematic. In any case, agreement from the course teacher(s) concerned should be obtained prior to the submission of the piece of work.

# **A Drop of Life (點滴是生命) \- CUHK Presentation 2026**

## **關於我們 (ABOUT A DROP OF LIFE)**

**We are a local Hong Kong NGO dedicated to solving the global water crisis.**

(我們是一家致力於解決全球水資源危機的香港本地非政府組織。)

Established in 1998, A Drop of Life takes the leads to implement sustainable water projects and build water facilities in Nepal, Cambodia, China and Myanmar.

(點滴是生命成立於 1998 年，率先在尼泊爾、柬埔寨、中國及緬甸推行永續的水資源專案並興建水資源設施。)

* **164,600+** WATER & SANITATION FACILITIES BUILT (已興建水資源與衛生設施)  
* **5,000,000+** PEOPLE GETTING CLEAN WATER (受惠獲得乾淨水資源的人數)

### **From Clean Water to Sustainable Future (從潔淨水源到永續未來)**

* Aligned with **SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS (SDGs)** (聯合國永續發展目標)  
* 主要關注點：**SDG 6 \- CLEAN WATER AND SANITATION** (淨水與衛生)

## **我們的旗艦活動 (OUR FLAGSHIP EVENT)**

### **RACE FOR WATER 背水一戰**

* A well-known **CHARITY TRAIL RUN** in Hong Kong with a 10-year history.  
  (香港擁有10年歷史的知名慈善越野跑活動。)  
* Continues to grow in popularity with increasing participation each year, engaging **2,000+ runners** and **400+ volunteers** every year.  
  (受歡迎程度持續增長，每年參與人數不斷增加，吸引超過 2,000 名跑者和 400 名志工參與。)  
* *活動形式：參賽者需揹負著水桶進行越野跑，體驗缺水地區居民取水的艱辛。*

## **面臨的挑戰 (THE CHALLENGE)**

### **1\. Hong Kong \- A Generous City, but a Crowded & Challenging Market (香港：慷慨的城市，但競爭激烈的市場)**

* **眾多知名 NGO 競爭：** UNICEF、THE COMMUNITY CHEST (香港公益金)、SPCA (愛護動物協會)、Habitat for Humanity (仁人家園)、World Vision (世界宣明會)、MSF (無國界醫生)、GREENPEACE (綠色和平)、WWF (世界自然基金會) 等。  
* **核心痛點：** We compete with giants. More critically, public trust in NGOs has fallen due to various scandals. Rebuilding trust while standing out is our dual challenge.  
  (我們與巨頭競爭。更關鍵的是，由於各種醜聞，公眾對非政府組織的信任度下降。在脫穎而出的同時重建信任是我們的雙重挑戰。)

### **2\. Water Scarcity \- A Problem That Feels Distant (水資源匱乏：一個感覺遙遠的問題)**

* People often donate only to causes that they can truly relate to.  
  (人們通常只捐款給他們能真正產生共鳴的事業。)  
* **核心痛點：** Beyond trust, how to make Hong Kongers relate to water problems and walking miles for water?  
  (除了信任之外，如何讓香港人對水資源問題以及「為水跋涉數英里」產生共鳴？)

## **COMM3400 專案任務 (PROJECT BRIEFS)**

### **PROJECT BRIEF 1: Bridging the Empathy Gap Across Generations (跨越世代的同理心鴻溝)**

A Drop of Life's current donor base is primarily **aged 30-60**, a group reachable through our established channels (Facebook, Instagram, EDM, WhatsApp). To ensure sustainable impact, we must understand and meaningfully engage two new audiences:

(點滴是生命目前的捐款者主要集中在 30-60 歲，這是一個可以透過我們現有管道接觸到的群體。為了確保永續的影響力，我們必須了解並有意義地吸引兩個新的受眾：)

**Target Audiences (目標受眾):**

1. **Gen Z (aged \~18-28) (Z世代):**  
   * How can we bridge the empathy gap on water issues and inspire this digitally native, socially conscious generation to take action and donate?  
     (我們如何彌合在水資源議題上的同理心鴻溝，並激勵這個數位原生、具社會意識的世代採取行動並捐款？)  
2. **The "Young-Old" (aged 60-75) (初老族群):**  
   * This demographic is often financially stable but may be overlooked. What campaign messaging and channels would most effectively reach and motivate them?  
     (這個族群通常財務穩定，但可能被忽視。什麼樣的活動訊息和管道能最有效地觸及並激勵他們？)

**The Task (任務要求):**

Develop a creative campaign concept tailored to these two audiences. Your goal is to make the global water crisis feel personally relevant and urgent to them, ultimately increasing their willingness to support A Drop of Life.

(為這兩個受眾量身打造一個創意的活動概念。您的目標是讓全球水資源危機對他們來說具有個人相關性和急迫性，最終提高他們支持點滴是生命的意願。)

**Deliverables (交付項目):**

* Audience insight summary & persona (受眾洞察總結與人物誌)  
* One big creative campaign idea (一個大型創意活動概念)  
* Proposed mix of promotion channels (建議的推廣管道組合)  
* Other relevant key asset (其他相關的關鍵資產)

### **PROJECT BRIEF 2: World Toilet Day Fundraising Campaign (世界廁所日募款活動)**

Access to clean water and proper sanitation are inseparable. One of our key projects in Cambodia is building toilets in villages, yet fundraising for this cause is challenging. We believe this is due to:

(獲得乾淨的水和適當的衛生設施是密不可分的。我們在柬埔寨的重點專案之一是為村莊建廁所，然而為此專案募款卻充滿挑戰。我們認為原因如下：)

* **Low awareness & discomfort (認知度低與不適感):** The topic of toilets is often seen as unglamorous, making it hard to promote, especially around World Toilet Day (Nov 19).  
  (廁所的話題通常被認為不具吸引力，使其難以推廣，尤其是在 11 月 19 日世界廁所日前後。)  
* **Perceived high cost (感知成本高):** A single donation of HK$3,500 to fund one toilet feels like a significant amount to many Hong Kong donors.  
  (捐款港幣 3,500 元資助一個廁所，對許多香港捐款者來說感覺是一筆不小的數目。)

**Case Study / Inspiration (案例參考):**

* **呂爵安 (Edan Lui) 粉絲生日應援：** 粉絲「爵屎」們為慶祝 Edan 生日，以「EDAN LUI」命名捐助建廁所，讓柬埔寨村民受惠。這個活動充滿趣味性（粉絲聯想「通渠CP」經歷），成功將慈善活動與流行文化結合，引起關注。

**The Task (任務要求):**

Design a creative advertising campaign that reframes the toilet as a gift of dignity, raises awareness about the sanitation crisis, and makes donating HK$3,500 feel accessible, meaningful, and even inspiring.

(設計一個具創意的廣告活動，將「廁所」重新包裝為「尊嚴的禮物」，提高對衛生危機的認識，並讓捐款 3,500 港元變得容易接受、有意義，甚至鼓舞人心。)

**Deliverables (交付項目):**

* One big creative campaign idea targeting World Toilet Day (針對世界廁所日的一個大型創意活動概念)  
* Proposed awareness and fundraising activation (建議的宣傳和募款啟動方案)  
* Proposed mix of promotion channels (建議的推廣管道組合)  
* Other relevant key asset (其他相關的關鍵資產)  
* Suggested donation mechanic/positioning for the HK$3,500 amount (針對港幣 3,500 元捐款額的建議捐款機制/定位)
